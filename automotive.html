Automotive Repair

About the qualification

  • Our online Responsible Service of Alcohol course is specially designed and contextualised for Queensland for those seeking employment in the industry.

  • Responsible Service of Alcohol (RSA) is mandatory training for hotel, bar and club staff. Our course provides people working in the hospitality industry with the knowledge and awareness that is required to responsibly sell, serve and supply alcohol in licensed venues

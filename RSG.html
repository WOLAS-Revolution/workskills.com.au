Responsible Service of Gambling Services (RSG)

SITHGAM201 Provide Responsible Gambling Services - National

About the qualification

Our online Responsible Service of Gaming course is specially designed and contextualised for those seeking to provide gambling services in Australia. After completing the Responsible Service of Gambling unit, you will be entitled to work in premises that have gaming machines and other gambling services.

To legally work in the gaming sector, you must hold a Nationally Recognised Responsible Gambling Services Certificate issued by an approved Registered Training Organisation.

Possible career pathways
  • Gaming attendant
  • Duty manager
  • Croupier

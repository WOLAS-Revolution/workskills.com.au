SITHFAB201 Provide Responsible Service of Alcohol

About the qualification

Responsible Service of Alcohol is mandatory training for hotel, bar and club staff. A Responsible Service of Alcohol course provides skills in handling difficult situations, for example, recognising the signs of alcohol intoxication, and using strategies to address them. This course also helps ensure you, the employee, are aware of your legal obligations and the ramifications associated with the service you provide.

Our online Responsible Service of Alcohol course is specially designed and contextualised for those seeking employment in the Australian Hospitality industry. Equipping them with the knowledge and awareness that is required to responsibly sell, serve and supply alcohol in a licensed venue.

Possible career pathways

  • Nightclub manager
  • Flight attendant

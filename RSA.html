Responsible Service of Alcohol

About the qualification

  • Responsible Service of Alcohol (RSA) is mandatory training for hotel, bar and club staff. A Responsible Service of Alcohol course provide skills in handling difficult situations, for example, recognising the signs of alcohol intoxication and problem gambling, and using strategies to address them. The courses also help ensure employees are aware of their legal obligations and the ramifications associated with the service they provide.

  • Our online Responsible Service of Alcohol course is specially designed and contextualised for Queensland for those seeking employment in the hospitality industry. with the knowledge and awareness that is required to responsibly sell, serve and supply alcohol in licensed venues.


Possible career pathways

  • Bartender
  • Nightclub manager
  • Flight attendant
  • Sommelier

Available Qualifications

  • SITHFAB201 - Provide Responsible Service of Alcohol

Average completion time:

  • 30 minutes, or at your own pace.

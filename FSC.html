Food Safety Supervisor Certificate (FSC)

Not valid in NSW

About the qualification

  • Licensed food businesses require a Food Safety Supervisor to be on staff at all times. The Food Safety Supervisor must hold a valid Statement of Attainment issued by a Registered Training Organisation (RTO), that's Work Skills, and we are with you all the way! If you have a question, then we have the answer.

  • Don't put the health of others at risk. Our Food Safety Supervisor Course will give you the skills and understanding to prepare, handle and serve food safely. This course also starts you off with two units in our Hospitality qualification.


Possible career pathways

  • Food Safety Supervisor.

Units of Competency

  • SITXFSA101 - Use Hygienic Practices for Food Safety
  • SITXFSA201 - Participate in Safe Food Handling Practices

Average completion time:

  • A few hours, or at your own pace.

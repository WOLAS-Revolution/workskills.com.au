Food Safety Supervisor Course

About the qualification

  • Licensed food businesses require a Food Safety Supervisor on staff at all times. The Food Safety Supervisor must hold a valid Statement of Attainment issued by a Registered Training Organisation (RTO).

  • Don't put the health of others at risk. Our Food Safety Supervisor course will give you the skills and understanding to prepare, handle and serve food safely. This course is also an excellent first step into the hospitality industry!

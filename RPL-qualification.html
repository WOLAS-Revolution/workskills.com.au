WHAT IS RPL?

Your qualification is primarily achieved through Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL).

The RPL process is easy. If you already have the skills and experience in the hospitality or automotive fields, we gather evidence and map it against the qualification requirements.

If there are any gaps in your skills or experience, or if evidence is missing, we can train and assess you in those areas for $1.60 per nominal hour of training.
